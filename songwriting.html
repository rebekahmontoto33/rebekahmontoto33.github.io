Since I can't offer you a song sample just yet, please enjoy and be encouraged by this poem.

"When we lose ourselves

And we can't go on

When we've given up

And we can't stand tall

When life gets hard

And faith depletes

That's when our prayers

Mean everything

When love just hurts

And pain feels right

Remember God

Is in our sight

Lift your voice

Into his word

And watch your heart

Fall to his good

You are not

And will never be alone

For our King watches over you

Upon the highest throne

Always pray

In spite of defeat

For our King

Conquers everything"
